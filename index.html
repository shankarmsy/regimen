Rules, Flags & Swaps
Absolute Avoids
  • Zero alcohol
  • Zero fruit juice
  • Zero sugar in tea or coffee
  • No food after 6:00pm
  • No jackfruit, litchi, mango
  • No Indian sweets
  • No supplements without GI clearance
  • PB max 1 tbsp/day
  • No caffeine after 1:00pm
Daily Non-Negotiables
  • Warm lemon + salt water 500ml on waking
  • Psyllium 1 tsp in water at 8:15am — chug before it gels
  • 10 min sunlight daily
  • Protein before carbs every meal
  • 15–20 min walk after dinner
  • Whey every single day
  • Cold shower finish
  • Metformin + fish oil with dinner
  • Rosuvastatin + losartan + magnesium at 9:30pm
Smart Swaps
  • Bored of eggs? Dal chilla
  • No chicken? Boiled eggs at dinner
  • Want crunch with rice? Roasted papad
  • Really want Lays? 1oz bag, not daily
  • Oatmeal boring? Cardamom + cinnamon
  • Rice too much? Poha instead
  • Banana urge rest day? Apple instead
  • CAVA day? Whey with water only
Water Hacks
  • 500ml bottle on nightstand
  • Phone alarms: 10:30am, 12pm, 3pm, 5pm
  • Pinch of salt + lime in morning water
  • Sparkling water counts for afternoon
  • Dark urine = drink immediately
  • Bring bottle to badminton every time
Liver Rules
  • Dinner always lighter than lunch
  • Ghee max 25g total/day
  • Check every OTC med with Dr. Chittajallu
  • AFP test every 6 months
  • ALT/AST labs every 3 months
  • Walnuts 10/day — liver-protective
  • Turmeric cooking=fine · capsules=ask first
Banana Rules
  • Only on workout/badminton/volleyball days
  • Post-exercise only (Sat pre-workout ok)
  • Never on rest days or run days
  • 1 small banana max per day
  • In oatmeal = counts as daily banana
  • Watermelon: 1 cup max, post-workout only
